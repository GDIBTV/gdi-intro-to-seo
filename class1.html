What is SEO?

SEO = Search Engine Optimization


Definition: "SEO is the process of affecting the visibility of a website or a web page in a search engine's unpaid results - often referred to as "natural," "organic," or "earned" results." - Wikipedia

Process - On-going monitoring effort.

Un-Paid - You can't pay Google to be #1.

SEO vs. SEM

SEM = Overall Marketing efforts on the search engines

SEO = Optimization efforts to improve visibility
Source: http://upcity.com/blog/seo-or-ppc/

SEO Terminology

Keyword / Search Query
A word or phrase that is entered into a search engine

Organic Listing
A result that shows on the search results page.

Ranking
The position of a listing within the search engine results
